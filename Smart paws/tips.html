Caring for Your Dog

  • Brush your dog's coat regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Use dog-friendly shampoo when giving baths.
  • Ensure they get daily exercise for a healthy life.
  • Provide a comfortable sleeping area.
  • Schedule regular vet check-ups to keep them healthy.
  • Give them chew toys to prevent destructive behavior.

Caring for Your Cat

  • Keep their litter box clean and in a quiet place.
  • Brush their fur regularly to prevent shedding.
  • Provide a scratching post to protect furniture.
  • Feed them a balanced diet with fresh water daily.
  • Give them cozy spots to nap and hide.
  • Spend time playing with them to keep them active.
